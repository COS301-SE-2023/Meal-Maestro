Sausage&Cheese Quiche
Save to Recipe Book

A delicious and easy to cook breakfast meal!

Preparation Time

• Prep Time: 30 min

Ingredients:

  • Sausage
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven at 180 and put the crust in dish and crimp edges.
  2. Cook and crumble sausage, then drain.
  3. Sprinkle sausage and cheese in pie crust.
  4. Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Pour over sausage and cheese.
  5. Bake for 30-35 mins until set and golden brown.
